Outlook on Windows (desktop)

  1. Fill in your details above.
  2. Click Copy Signature.
  3. In Outlook: File → Options → Mail → Signatures…
  4. Create or select a signature, then paste (Ctrl + V) into the editor.

Outlook on the web (Office 365)

  1. Open Outlook on the web and click the gear icon (⚙).
  2. Go to Mail → Compose and reply.
  3. Under Email signature, click into the editor.
  4. Return to this page and click Copy Signature.
  5. Go back to Outlook on the web and paste (Ctrl + V or ⌘ + V).
  6. Choose when to use the signature (for new messages / replies) and click Save.

Outlook mobile (iOS / Android)

  1. Open the Outlook app → Settings → Signature.
  2. Mobile apps often support only simple text or limited formatting.
  3. You may paste the signature, but complex styling may be simplified.
  4. For best results, keep the mobile signature shorter (for example, your name, title, and your role).
